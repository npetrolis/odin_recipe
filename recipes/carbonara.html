Simple Carbonara Recipe

Description

Carbonara is an Italian classic. It's a simple yet flavorful dish made with pasta, egg yolks, guanciale, pecorino romano, parmeggiano reggiano and pepper

Ingredients

  • 250g of your preferred pasta, we recomment Linguini
  • 2-3 Egg Yolks
  • 100g Guanciale
  • 40g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 60g Parmegiano Reggiano
  • Freshly Ground Pepper

Steps

  1. Add the pasta to salted boiling water
  2. Cook the pasta for two minutes less than the time specified at the packaging
  3. Start cooking the guanciale on a lightly oiled pan
  4. Separate your egg whites and egg yolks, you'll need 2 to 3 egg yolks per 250g of pasta
  5. Beat the yolks and add 2/3rds of the cheese, keep stirring until homogenous then add pepper
  6. Once the pasta and guanciale are cooked, kill the heat and add the pasta in the guanciale pan
  7. Stir them together and add your egg-cheese mixture, keep stirring the pasta for 1 to 2 minutes for the sauce to bond with your pasta
