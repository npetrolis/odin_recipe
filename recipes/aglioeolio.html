Aglio e Olio Recipe

Description

Aglio e Olio is a traditional Italian pasta dish. It only uses garlic, parmeggiano reggiano cheese, olive oil, parsley and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 250g of your preferred pasta, we recomment Linguini
  • 2-3 Garlic Cloves
  • 2-3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 100g Parmegiano Reggiano
  • Freshly Ground Pepper
  • Freshly Chopped Parsley

Steps

  1. Add the pasta to salted boiling water
  2. Cook the pasta for two minutes less than the time specified at the packaging
  3. Start cooking the garlic on a pan with your olive oil over medium-low heat
  4. Once the pasta is ready, kill the heat and add the pasta in the garlic pan
  5. Add some pasta water, the parsley, about one half of the cheese and stir for 2 minutes
  6. Serve on a dish and add the rest of the Parmegiano Reggiano
